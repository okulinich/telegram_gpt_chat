THE PROCESS

STEP 1 - CONTACT (1 WEEK)

PRELIMINARY DISCUSSION


We will analyze your needs and the scope of the planned facility over the phone or in person. Among other things, we will discuss your specific project goals to determine whether a KOLEO solution is right for you. Among other things, we will discuss – Budget – Timing – Scope – Logistics

STEP 2 – DESIGN AND ENGINEERING (3-6 WEEKS)

1.STANDARD PLAN/STANDARD PLAN WITH MODIFICATIONS OR INDIVIDUAL PROJECT

Under this option, KOLEO will provide a standard plan or modify a standard plan with the specified components, as well as provide visualization of your home, a 3D design, and a full construction specification and cost of building your home kit. Once you have finalized the drawings, we can proceed with filling out the permits for submission to the local building authority.

2.INDIVIDUAL FLOOR PLAN, DRAWINGS FOR OBTAINING PERMITS AND DESIGNING A HOUSE

KOLEO will provide a custom design and custom of house that can be submitted for local permit approvals. – Design Brief and Proposal – Conceptual Design and floorplans – Architectural Drawing Set – Custom Foundation Engineering – Price is quoted per project

STEP 3 – GET IN THE QUEUE (1 Week-6 Months)

1.RESERVATION OF THE PRICE, START OF HOUSE KIT ASSEMBLY

To start the production of a house kit, you need to make a deposit in the amount of the house sale price. Production will take up to 3 months.

2.SUBMIT FOR PERMIT

Permit documents are submitted to the local municipality for approval. – The cost of the permit can vary depending on the country of the recipient, the municipality and the size of the building – the time to obtain the permit can vary greatly depending on the municipality chosen.

STEP 4 – BUILD (1-3 Months)

1.PRODUCTION

Production and molding of the house kit begins – Weekly photos and updates are sent via a private web page – Completion date is updated.

2.COMPLETION OF FACTORY PRODUCTION

The house kit is ready for transportation to the customer’s country. Negotiation of convenient delivery time.

STEP 5 – RECEIVING A HOUSE KIT (1-6 WEEKS)

1.SHIPPING AND DELIVERY

The house set is packed in a truck and sent to the destination (the territory of the European Union). For delivery to another continent (North and South America, Asia, etc.), the truck with the house set goes to the port and delivery is organized by cargo ship.

2.INTERNAL LOGISTICS

The truck will deliver the house set within the European Union directly to the place you have specified.
When the goods arrive at the seaport, we can arrange domestic delivery or leave it to the customer to decide.

Glued laminated wood is a very innovative building material. Glulam is stronger than steel and offers greater strength and stiffness than dimensional lumber of similar size. Its increased design value, product performance, and cost competitiveness make it an excellent choice for projects ranging from simple beams and headers in residential construction to towering arches in dome roofs spanning 150 meters or more.

Glulam is a stress-rated engineered wood beam composed of durable, moisture-resistant, adhesive-bonded wood laminates (“lams”). The grain of the laminate runs parallel to the length of the member. From simple straight beams to complex curved beams, Glulam is versatile. Available in custom and stock sizes, and in four appearance options: premium, architectural, industrial, and framing.

GLUED LAMINATED TIMBER

WHAT IS GLULAM?

Glued-laminated timber (glulam) is a structural engineered wood products are commonly used for beams and columns in residential and commercial buildings. In contemporary projects, it has become a highly sought after form of mass timber, framed in long spans to take advantage of the natural beauty of the wood and expose its distinctive design.

Versatile engineered wood product delivers strength, beauty and reliability.
